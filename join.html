Join Us

Join the Founding Team of a New Scientific Discipline

The BIA Lab is new. The people who join now aren't just members. They are founding scientists of a field that is only now becoming possible.

We are looking for people who want to develop the Computational Architecture of Living Systems.
Who We're Looking For

Mindset over background

We welcome people from any quantitative field. What we look for is the kind of person who, when told something can't be done, asks why not? and then goes and figures it out. People energised by problems without solutions, who treat "we don't know" as an invitation.

You want to invent, not just apply. You reach for first principles when a tool doesn't exist yet.

You are drawn to hard, messy data. Terabyte-scale imaging and biological noise excite rather than deter you.

You care about biological meaning. The computation is a means. Understanding how cells encode and transmit decisions is the end.

You finish things. Ideas are cheap; tools that thousands of researchers use are not.

Equally Important

BIA is probably not for you if…

We'd rather you know this now than six months in.

This is probably not the right fit if you…

  • Want a narrowly scoped project with a clear, pre-defined outcome
  • Prefer incremental refinement of established methods over inventing new ones
  • Are uncomfortable with ambiguity or frequently changing research directions

None of these are flaws. They describe many excellent scientists working in the right environment for them.

Process

How to apply

We keep first contact simple. Strong candidates are contacted within two weeks.

1

Read our work and imagine what you could build here

Explore our publications and tools. Ask yourself: where could you push the needle?

2

Send two things

CV and a 1-page research vision: what you want to work on and why BIA is the right place. That is all we need to start a conversation. Referees and research statements come after interview.

3

Use the form or email directly

Submit below, or email felix.y.zhou@vanderbilt.edu with subject [Position] – Application.

4

Interview

A 45-minute conversation to find genuine mutual fit. Finalists are invited for an on-site visit.
