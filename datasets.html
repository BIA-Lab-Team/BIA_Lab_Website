Open Data Resources

Curated, reusable datasets from BIA Lab publications — released for the community to build on.

Each dataset listed here is a direct output of a lab publication and has been released in a form intended for independent reuse. Format, scale, and access information are summarised on each card. If you use any of these datasets in your work, please cite the associated paper — citations are the primary currency of academic science and directly support the lab's ability to secure funding and continue producing open resources for the community.

Human Tumor 3D Immune Microenvironment Atlas

● Open
Yapp C, Nirmal AJ, Zhou F et al. · Nature Methods 2025

Highly multiplexed 3D cyclic immunofluorescence (CyCIF) imaging of human tumor tissue, enabling spatial profiling of cell states and immune niches at single-cell resolution in three dimensions. Hosted through the Human Tumor Atlas Network (HTAN) tissue-atlas portal.

3D Spatial Proteomics Multiplexed Imaging OME-TIFF Human FFPE Tissue
Access Dataset ↗ View Paper

u-segment3D Example Data — High-Resolution Light-Sheet Imaging

● Open
Zhou FY et al. · Nature Methods 2025

High-resolution light-sheet fluorescence microscopy volumes of cells exhibiting complex protrusive morphologies — blebs, filopodia, and lamellipodia — used to benchmark and demonstrate the u-segment3D universal 3D cell segmentation pipeline. Provided as example data accompanying the software release.

3D Microscopy Light-Sheet FLSM tar.gz · 1.8 GB CC BY 4.0
Access Dataset ↗ View Paper u-segment3D Tool

Endoscopy Artefact Detection (EAD) Dataset

● Open
Ali S†, Zhou F† et al. · Scientific Reports 2020 † Joint first authors Ali S, Zhou F et al. · arXiv 2019

Multi-centre benchmark dataset for artefact detection and segmentation in clinical endoscopy, aggregated from six facilities across the UK, France, Italy, Switzerland, and Russia. Covers gastroscopy, cystoscopy, and colonoscopy across white-light, fluorescence, and narrow-band imaging modalities. Supports three challenge tasks: multi-class bounding-box detection, region segmentation, and cross-domain generalisation.

Endoscopy Imaging Detection & Segmentation Multi-centre · 6 sites CC BY NC 3.0
Access Dataset ↗ View Papers

Normal 2-Cell Population Migration Videos

● Open
Zhou FY, Ruiz-Puig C, Owen RP, White MJ, Rittscher J, Lu X · eLife 2019

Time-lapse microscopy videos of two co-cultured cell populations undergoing collective migration, used to develop and validate the Motion Sensing Superpixels (MOSES) computational framework for quantifying and discovering cellular motion phenotypes.

Cell Migration Time-lapse Video Microscopy · Video CC BY 4.0
Access Dataset ↗ View Paper MOSES Tool

EGF Addition to EPC2:CP-A Cell Migration Videos

● Open
Zhou FY, Ruiz-Puig C, Owen RP, White MJ, Rittscher J, Lu X · eLife 2019

Time-lapse microscopy videos documenting the response of EPC2 and CP-A oesophageal cell lines to EGF stimulation, capturing changes in collective migration phenotype used to benchmark the MOSES motion analysis pipeline.

Cell Migration Time-lapse Video Microscopy · Video CC BY 4.0
Access Dataset ↗ View Paper MOSES Tool
